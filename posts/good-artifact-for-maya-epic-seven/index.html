EPIC SEVEN Maya Hero Review - Best Builds and Artifacts for a Good Artifact for Maya

Maya is a 4-star hero in the popular mobile game EPIC SEVEN. In this guide, we will review Maya’s skills, analyze the best items and sets for her, and discuss the ideal artifact builds for this F2P hero. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced player, this article will provide you with all the information you need to maximize Maya’s potential in your lineup.

Maya’s Skills Review#

Before discussing Maya’s optimal builds, let’s take a closer look at her skills.

Skill 1: Blunt Weapon

Maya’s basic attack has a 25% chance to stun the target for one turn. The chance to stun increases by 10% if the target’s health is below 50%. However, this skill does not work on bosses.

Skill 2: Soul Thrust

This skill deals damage to a single target and has a 35% chance to provoke all enemies for one turn. It also increases the defense of all allies for five turns. The provoke chance can increase to 50% with skill enhancements.

Skill 3: Defensive Formation

Maya’s ultimate skill reduces the attack of all enemies for two turns with a 100% chance. It also increases the defense of all allies for two turns and grants a shield to the entire team for five turns. The cooldown for this skill is six turns and can be reduced to five turns with skill enhancements.

Best Items and Sets for Maya#

To maximize Maya’s potential, it is important to equip her with the right items and sets. Here are the recommended options:

Items: Focus on increasing Maya’s defense, speed, and effectiveness.

  • Defense: Maya’s skills scale with defense, so prioritize items that boost her defense stat.
  • Speed: Maya’s speed stat plays a crucial role in determining her turn order. Equip her with items that increase her speed to ensure she acts before the enemy.
  • Effectiveness: To increase the chances of landing her debuffs, such as stun and provoke, focus on items that enhance Maya’s effectiveness.

Sets: There are several sets that work well with Maya. Here are the top recommendations:

  • Triple Defense Set: Equipping Maya with three defense sets will significantly increase her overall defense, making her more durable in battles.
  • Defense and Hit Set: If you have access to high-quality hit gear from Wyvern hunts, consider using a defense and hit set combination. This will increase her effectiveness and improve her chances of landing debuffs.

Best Artifact Builds for Maya#

Selecting the right artifact can greatly enhance Maya’s performance on the battlefield. Here are the top artifact options for this hero:

1. Proof of Valor

Proof of Valor is an excellent choice for Maya, as it reduces her skill cooldowns by 10% at the beginning of battle. This can provide a significant advantage, especially in longer battles, by allowing Maya to use her skills more frequently.

2. Prophetic Candlestick

Prophetic Candlestick is another strong artifact for Maya. With a 40% chance to reduce skill cooldowns by one turn when attacked, Maya can benefit from more frequent skill usage in combat. This artifact is especially useful in situations where Maya is taking most of the hits, such as in the front line of a team composition.

3. Other Options

While Proof of Valor and Prophetic Candlestick are the top choices for Maya, you can also consider other artifacts that increase her overall survivability or improve her debuff potential.

Step-by-Step Guide for Enhancing Maya’s Skills#

Now that we have reviewed the best items, sets, and artifacts for Maya, let’s discuss the optimal order for enhancing her skills.

  1. Prioritize Ultimate Skill: Maya’s ultimate skill, Defensive Formation, should be your top priority. Enhancing this skill will reduce the cooldown from six turns to five turns, allowing Maya to use it more frequently in battles.

  2. Secondary Skills: After enhancing Maya’s ultimate, focus on her second skill, Soul Thrust. Increasing the provoke chance from 35% to 50% will make her more effective in controlling enemies.

  3. Basic Attack Enhancement: Finally, you can enhance Maya’s basic attack, Blunt Weapon, to increase the stun chance from 25% to 35%. While this skill does not work on bosses, it can still be handy in controlling smaller enemies in Adventures or Abyss.

Conclusion#

Maya is a versatile hero in EPIC SEVEN, capable of stunning, provoking, and debuffing enemies. By equipping her with the right items, sets, and artifacts, you can further enhance her skills and make her a valuable asset to your team. Remember to prioritize her ultimate skill and choose artifacts that increase her survivability or debuff potential. With the right build, Maya can become a force to be reckoned with in various game modes.

Remember to keep grinding for the items and sets mentioned in this guide to enhance Maya’s potential and unlock her true power!
