How to Unlock Alt. Ultimate Arena Pad and Clear Alt. Arena 3

Alt. Arena 3 is a challenging dungeon in the popular mobile game, Puzzle & Dragons. In this article, we will guide you on how to unlock and clear Alt. Arena 3 using the powerful combination of Alice and Fasca. This strategy requires careful planning and execution, as well as a deep understanding of the game mechanics.

Step 1: Unlocking Alt. Ultimate Arena Pad#

To unlock Alt. Ultimate Arena Pad, you will need to complete certain requirements:

  1. Reach rank 400 in the game.
  2. Clear all of the previous Ultimate Arena dungeons, including Alt. Arena 1 and 2.
  3. Complete the specific quest given to unlock Alt. Arena 3.

Once you have fulfilled these requirements, Alt. Arena 3 will become available for you to challenge.

Step 2: Building your Team#

For this dungeon, you will need a team of powerful monsters with specific abilities. The team used in the video consists of Alice and Fasca as the leaders, accompanied by other utility monsters.

Alice has the ability to resist all damage types, making her an excellent choice for this dungeon. Fasca, on the other hand, provides additional skill boosts, which will be crucial for strategic gameplay.

Step 3: Executing the Strategy#

As shown in the video, timing and skill activation are essential for successfully clearing Alt. Arena 3. Follow these steps to maximize your chances of success:

  1. Activate Alice’s skill first, as she will transform into her more powerful state.
  2. Immediately after, activate Fasca’s skill, as she will not transform unless you press the button. This will ensure you have the maximum number of skill boosts.
  3. Utilize the extra orbs generated by Alice’s and Fasca’s skills to make three light combos. These combos will give you an auto follow-up attack and additional damage.
  4. Avoid pushing enemies into execute range by ensuring you have three light combos. One light combo can be fatal, leading to a failed run.
  5. Be mindful of the time limit, as Alt. Arena 3 provides only ten seconds of movement time. This can be stressful, especially on a 7x6 board. Plan your moves carefully to avoid running out of time.

Step 4: Clearing the Floors#

Alt. Arena 3 consists of multiple floors, each with its own challenges. Here is a brief overview of each floor and important points to note:

  1. Floor 1: Make sure to have enough light combos for damage and auto follow-up attack.
  2. Floor 2: Use Chidori to generate light orbs and increase your light up stat. Aim for two additional combos for more damage.
  3. Floor 3: Stalling is possible due to the enemy’s two-turn skill activation. Plan accordingly and avoid using unnecessary skills.
  4. Floor 4: Watch out for resolve status. Use Brad Amante to clear it before proceeding.
  5. Floor 5: Use Ariel to remove Halloween Pumpkin, which is no longer needed. Charge Ariel by matching orbs.
  6. Floor 6: Prepare for the largest preemptive damage in the dungeon. Make sure your team can survive it.
  7. Floor 7: Use Chidori and other skills to deal damage and reach the combo threshold for auto follow-up attacks.
  8. Floor 8: Carefully manage your light orbs to ensure you have enough for VDP (Void Damage Piercer) and additional combos.
  9. Floor 9: Be aware of the blinding effect. Make sure to have full blind resistance to mitigate its impact.
  10. Floor 10: Utilize your light combos and skills to deal massive damage and clear the final floor.

Conclusion#

Unlocking and clearing Alt. Arena 3 requires careful planning, execution, and a strong team. By following the strategy outlined in this article, you can increase your chances of success and conquer this challenging dungeon. Remember to adapt your team and strategy based on your own monster collection and playstyle. Good luck, and have fun in Alt. Arena 3!
