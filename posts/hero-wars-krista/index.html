Introduction#

In this episode of “Let’s Play Hero Wars,” we are introduced to the new hero, Krista. Join us as we explore her abilities, test her in battles, and see how she can be integrated into our teams. Krista is an exciting addition to the Hero Wars roster, and we can’t wait to see what she brings to the game.

Arena Battles#

We start off the video with some arena battles, aiming to quickly complete our fights before running out of time. We win some battles and encounter challenging opponents with strong heroes. We strategize, relying on silencing and stunning skills to overcome our enemies. Though we face defeat in some battles, we remain determined to improve our ranking.

Completing Quests#

To progress further, we decide to focus on completing quests. We prioritize energy collection, soul expeditions, and improving the strength of our heroes. We also take a moment to check our guild mates and interact with them. Our guild plays an essential role in our Hero Wars journey, and we appreciate the support and camaraderie.

Acquiring Krista#

Unexpectedly, we acquire the new hero, Krista. Although we initially intended to save our resources, we decide to make the most of the opportunity and integrate Krista into our lineup. We discuss the potential benefits of having Krista on our team and speculate on whether she counts as souls for specific purposes.

Glyphs and Skills#

After acquiring Krista, we turn our attention to upgrading glyphs and improving the skills of our heroes. We prioritize marksman heroes and support heroes, aiming to enhance their magic attack abilities. We take advantage of the available resources, such as freebies and energy, to optimize our hero’s potential.

Guild War and Cards#

We participate in the guild war and unfortunately face defeat. Though it happens frequently, we remain determined to improve our strategies and eventually claim victory. We receive cards and energy as rewards, which will aid us in our hero’s growth.

Progression and Future Plans#

Assessing our current progress, we evaluate our options for future advancement. We consider purchasing the Giant Slayer and farming various resources. Additionally, we plan for future events, such as Titan Spheres discounts and upcoming time-limited events. We strategize on when to use our resources wisely for maximum benefits.

Leveling Krista and Others#

With ample resources at our disposal, we decide to focus on leveling Krista. We use experience potions and allocate skill points to elevate her abilities. However, we exercise caution to ensure we don’t waste valuable resources. We discuss the potential benefits of including Krista in our Grand Arena teams and consider her as a potential second tank.

Utilizing Consumables and Upgrades#

We review our consumables and determine the best course of action to optimize their usage. We upgrade heroes’ equipment and explore various options to enhance their power. We strategize for future events and challenges, considering factors such as rarity of items and potential skill upgrades.

Grand Arena Battles#

We participate in Grand Arena battles, where our newly aligned teams face off against tough opponents. Our strategies involve utilizing the strengths of our heroes to counter the opponents’ abilities. Although we face fierce competition, we manage to secure some wins and continue improving our ranks.

Conclusion#

In this episode of “Let’s Play Hero Wars,” we introduced the new hero, Krista, and explored her abilities and potential. We engaged in arena battles, completed quests, upgraded our heroes, and strategized for future events and challenges. With Krista on our team, we are excited to see how she contributes to the game and enhances our gameplay. Join us in the next episode as we continue our Hero Wars journey and strive for victory.
