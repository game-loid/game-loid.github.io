Mage PvE Class Guide: Everything You Need to Know About Ulala Mage Skills

Introduction#

In this article, we will be discussing the mage class in Ulala Idle Adventure, a popular mobile game. The mage class, also known as the “meme master,” is a powerful and versatile class that excels in dealing damage, stunning enemies, and providing utility to the team. Whether you are new to the mage class or want to optimize your build for PvE content, this guide has got you covered. So let’s dive in and explore the mage skills and builds in great detail.

Why You Should Play Mage#

The mage class offers a unique playstyle that attracts many players. With their impressive damage output, stunning abilities, and utility skills, mages are a force to be reckoned with. They excel in both single-target and area-of-effect (AoE) damage, making them valuable assets in any PvE scenario. Additionally, mages have various utility skills such as purification wave, mirror image, and silence, which add versatility to their gameplay. If you want to dominate the game and contribute significantly to your team’s success, the mage class is an excellent choice.

Mage Builds#

  1. Leveling Build: For the first island, it is recommended to go with a 3-0-2 build. As you progress further, you can switch to a 3-1-1 or 3-2-0 build. These builds provide a good balance of damage and critical chance.

  2. Single Target DPS Build: If your primary focus is dealing raw damage to single targets, this build is for you. It consists of using fire blast, scorch, lightning blast, and fireball in sequence. Fire blast increases your critical chance, scorch double casts when it crits, lightning blast ignores defense, and fireball deals maximum damage when the target has low HP.

  3. Tank and Support Build: When you don’t need more DPS and want to provide support to your team, this build comes in handy. It involves using frost missile, kona cold, frost bolt, and serenity to stun enemies multiple times, giving relief to the tank and healers. With this build, you can stun the boss for a significant portion of the fight, making it highly useful in challenging encounters.

  4. AFK Build: If you prefer an AFK build for auto-battling, which aims to kill all mobs in the first 20 seconds, use sealed storm, thermal storm, meteorite, and light wave. The combination of these abilities ensures maximum damage output and helps you achieve the maximum XP threshold.

  5. Utility Build: Mages have several utility skills that make them incredibly versatile. Use abilities like purification wave, mirror image, and silence strategically to strip buffs, stun enemies, and interrupt their abilities. Utility builds make you a valuable asset to your team in both PvE and PvP scenarios.

Wishes and Card Builds#

When it comes to wishes, it is recommended to hold onto your wish coins as mages require dynamic builds based on your needs. The mage class excels in various aspects of the game, such as PvE, single-target damage, AoE damage, and PvP, and you may need to fill in the gaps as you progress. Therefore, it is better to save your wish coins and spend them when you have a specific need.

For card builds, a bruiser build is recommended for players who are lighter spenders or free-to-play. This build focuses on having a balance between attack and mitigation. For example, when using sets like Giant John or Volcano, aim for a 50/50 split between attack and mitigation cards. This approach ensures a good balance between dealing damage and surviving tough encounters.

Conclusion#

The mage class in Ulala Idle Adventure offers players a thrilling and versatile gameplay experience. With their impressive damage output, stunning abilities, and utility skills, mages are a valuable asset in any team. Whether you are focusing on PvE content, single-target damage, or utility support, there is a mage build for you. By following the recommended builds and utilizing the various mage skills effectively, you can maximize your damage output, stun enemies, and provide valuable utility to your team. So go ahead, choose the mage class, and embark on an epic adventure in the world of Ulala!
