Step-by-Step Guide to Acquiring a 7-Star Hero#

  1. Consistent Engagement: Make it a part of your routine to log into Epic Summoners twice a day, once in the morning and again in the evening, to optimize your progress and stay committed to your goal.

  2. Save Resources: As you progress in the game, avoid spending experience points (XP), money, or any other valuable resources on heroes that you plan to use for reaching the 7-Star Hero level. Instead, preserve these resources for later stages of the game when they will prove more useful.

  3. Reach Level 140: Once your hero reaches level 140, a fourth bar will appear, indicating that you are ready to proceed towards obtaining a 7-Star Hero.
