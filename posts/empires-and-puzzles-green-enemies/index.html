Top 10 Heroes to Limit Break in Green Empires and Puzzles: A Comprehensive Guide

July 19, 2023 · 6 min · 1104 words · Sandra Barnard

In the world of Empires and Puzzles, choosing the right heroes to limit break can make a significant difference in your gameplay. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just starting out, it’s essential to understand which heroes are worth investing in. In this guide, we will discuss the top 10 heroes to limit break in the green category. We will cover both five-star and lower star heroes, providing you with a comprehensive overview of their strengths and weaknesses.

Before we dive into the heroes, let’s briefly discuss the concept of limit breaking. Limit breaking is a feature in Empires and Puzzles that allows you to enhance your heroes’ stats and abilities beyond their usual limits. By doing this, you can increase their power and effectiveness in battles.

Now, let’s get to the heroes. We will be grading them on a scale ranging from C to A+, with A+ being the highest rating. Keep in mind that these rankings are subjective and based on our experience and analysis.

C-range Heroes

  1. Caitlin: Caitlin is a hero who primarily deals damage to all enemies at a fast speed. While she can provide some defense against special skills, overall, she is underwhelming compared to other heroes.

  2. Alkanin: Alkanin’s ability is to deal damage to nearby enemies and increase healing. However, this increase in healing is not significant enough to make him a standout hero.

  3. Guardian Chameleon: This hero can be useful against Mythic Titans, but other than that, he doesn’t offer many exceptional qualities.

B-Range Heroes

  1. Lady Locke: Lady Locke deals damage over four turns and dispels status ailments from allies. While her damage starts off low, she can be an asset in battles.

  2. Horghall: Horghall deals damage to all enemies and reduces their attack for a duration. His key strength lies in his attack reduction ability, which can be beneficial for six turns.

  3. Zocc: Zocc is a hero who deals damage to a single target and potentially sets up an increase in mana for that target. However, his abilities can be a bit messy and lack focus.

  4. Elradir: Elradir deals damage to nearby enemies and has a chance to reflect status ailments. While he has some promising traits, he doesn’t see much play outside of specific situations.

  5. Horghall Costume: Horghall’s costume provides additional attack decrease, making him a better version of his regular self.

B+ Heroes

  1. Morgan Le Fay: Morgan Le Fay deals fast damage while draining life and increasing her defense against fire. While she used to be a formidable hero, recent power creep has diminished her impact.

  2. Gregorion: Gregorion is primarily used against Titans due to his critical chance ability. However, he doesn’t excel in other areas of the game.

  3. Margaret: Margaret received a buff that improved her abilities, but she still falls short compared to other dodge-focused heroes.

  4. Lady of the Lake: Lady of the Lake is widely used in very fast wars because her minion reduction ability is incredibly frustrating for opponents.

  5. Jade: Jade is an underwhelming ninja hero who relies on dodge mechanics but lacks damage-dealing capabilities.

  6. Telluria: Telluria was once a top-tier hero but has been heavily nerfed. While she still serves as a decent tank, she lacks offensive capabilities.

  7. Lady of the Lake Costume: This version of Lady of the Lake provides negative mana generation, making her even more annoying to face in battles.

  8. The Hatter: The Hatter is a useful hero for stealing buffs, but beyond that, he doesn’t offer much.

  9. Tarlak: Tarlak is primarily used against titans due to his damage increase ability. However, he doesn’t offer much flexibility outside of that specific role.

A-Range Heroes

  1. Lianna: Lianna deals massive damage to a single target and can be devastating when limit broken.

  2. Zeline: Zeline is a versatile hero who dispels buffs from enemies and deals damage with an attack decrease.

  3. Atomos: Atomos got a buff that improved his abilities, but he is still not commonly seen in gameplay.

  4. Lianna Costume: Lianna’s costume provides her with additional mana generation reduction, making her a stronger version of her regular self.

  5. Elkanen Costume: Elkanen’s costume deals damage to targets and nearby enemies, along with providing substantial healing gain.

  6. Gargantuar: Gargantuar deals damage and provides regeneration over three turns. While useful, he doesn’t stand out as a top-tier hero.

  7. Eiora & Fluffy: This hero excels at dealing damage to minions and reducing minion summoning. With the rise of minion heroes, Eiora & Fluffy could become even more valuable.

  8. Francine Costume: Francine’s costume allows her to deal damage to targets and nearby enemies while offering cleansing abilities and defensive down effects.

  9. Kongalak: Kongalak is a new hero who shows promise but needs further evaluation due to his slow speed.

  10. Evelyn: Evelyn deals damage to targets and nearby enemies, dispels buffs, and provides nature defense down. She is a versatile hero with multiple beneficial abilities.

Three-Star and Four-Star Heroes

For those on a budget or starting out in the game, here are a few three-star and four-star heroes worth considering:

  1. Ishtak Costume: This hero deals damage to targets and nearby enemies while reducing defense.

  2. Grevle: Grevle’s health-boosting ability can be valuable in wars and offers an attack increase.

  3. Berden Costume: Berden’s costume deals damage and reduces the defense of targets for an extended duration.

  4. Carver: Carver deals damage to all enemies and reduces their mana, making him a surprising contender.

Choosing the right heroes to limit break in Empires and Puzzles can greatly impact your gameplay. Consider the abilities and strengths of each hero before making your decision. Experiment with different combinations and strategies to find the ones that suit your playstyle best.
