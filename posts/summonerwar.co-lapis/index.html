Introduction#

In the popular mobile game Summoners War, players are constantly seeking ways to improve their gameplay and enhance their strategies. One crucial aspect of the game is effectively running characters, and in this article, we will dive deep into how to efficiently rune one specific character - Lapis. Here, you will find step-by-step instructions and valuable tips to maximize Lapis’ potential and elevate your gaming experience.

Understanding Lapis and its Importance#

Lapis is a powerful water-type monster in Summoners War, known for her versatile and adaptable skills. She possesses the ability to inflict significant damage on opponents, especially in PvP battles. However, to unleash her full potential, it is crucial to rune her effectively. By doing so, you can optimize her strengths and create a strong and reliable ally in your team.

Step 1: Focus on Lapis’ Skills and Abilities#

Before diving into the rune recommendations, it is essential to understand Lapis’ skills and how they contribute to her performance. Lapis possesses several unique abilities that make her a formidable force on the battlefield:

  1. Sword of Promise (Passive): This passive skill grants Lapis a 25% chance to stun enemies for one turn with each attack. It is essential to maximize this skill’s potential by choosing runes that enhance her attack speed and overall performance.

  2. Magic Shot (Skill 1): Lapis fires a magical arrow at the enemy, inflicting damage. This skill has a 30% chance to decrease the enemy’s attack bar by 15%. Powerful rune choices will ensure that this skill hits harder and increases the chances of diminishing the enemy’s turn meter.

  3. Spirit Shot (Skill 2): Lapis attacks the enemy with an icy storm, inflicting damage. This skill has an additional 50% chance to strike again with a weaker attack. Rune choices should focus on increasing critical damage and ensuring high accuracy for this skill.

  4. Hellfire (Skill 3): Lapis uses her mana to attack the enemy with overwhelming power, inflicting heavy damage. This skill is Lapis’ main damage-dealing ability, and the selected runes must amplify her attack power and critical damage.

Step 2: Rune Recommendations#

To rune Lapis effectively, you need to choose the most suitable runes for her abilities. Here are our recommended rune sets for Lapis:

  1. Fatal + Blade (Attack%, Critical Rate%, Attack%): This rune set focuses on maximizing Lapis’ attack power and critical rate, allowing her to deal massive damage. The additional attack% will further increase her damage output, making her a formidable attacker.

  2. Vampire + Revenge (Attack%, Critical Damage%, Attack%): This rune set provides Lapis with sustainability and allows her to recover health with each attack. The critical damage% rune ensures her attacks are even more devastating, making her an excellent option for prolonged battles.

Step 3: Rune Substats and Enhancements#

To further improve Lapis’ performance, pay attention to the substats on her runes. Aim for the following substats:

  1. Speed: Increasing Lapis’ speed allows her to act more frequently, increasing her chances of stunning opponents and reducing their attack bar. Look for substats that increase her speed whenever possible.

  2. Critical Rate: Boosting Lapis’ critical rate ensures that her attacks land critical hits more frequently, maximizing her damage potential. Prioritize substats that enhance critical rate.

  3. Attack: Substats that increase Lapis’ attack power directly contribute to her overall damage output. Look for runes with high attack substats to maximize her offensive capabilities.

  4. Accuracy: Lapis’ skills heavily rely on landing debuffs and reducing the enemy’s attack bar. Improve her accuracy through substats to ensure her skills have a higher chance of successfully landing the desired effects.

Conclusion#

Effectively running Lapis is a crucial aspect of achieving success in Summoners War. By following the step-by-step guidelines provided in this article, you can optimize Lapis’ abilities and create a powerful ally. Remember to focus on her key skills, choose the appropriate rune set, and enhance her substats wisely. So, don’t wait any longer, apply these tips, and dominate the battlefield with Lapis as your trusted companion in Summoners War!
