Epic Seven Nightmare Raid - The Ultimate Guide to Clearing All Bosses

Are you struggling to clear the Nightmare Asthma Callous raid in Epic Seven? Look no further. In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step process to create your own 100% clear raid team. These teams are reliable, fast, and will make your monthly grind much easier. So, let’s get started!

Introduction#

Before we dive into the team compositions, let’s go over a few alternatives to the units mentioned in this guide. If you don’t have access to the specific units, be sure to check out the description below for links to alternative team compositions. Additionally, we have provided a cheat sheet for your convenience.

Team Composition#

We will begin by discussing the most rewarding fight, Queen Kise as a Magic. This team composition may seem a little unusual, but it is incredibly stable and easy to run.

  1. Tamarin - Tamarin is a crucial unit for this fight. Her kit seems tailor-made for Queen Kise. Make sure she has 105 Effectiveness, as this will ensure successful debuffing. Additionally, aim for 200 Effect Resistance to prevent your Soul Weaver from being controlled.

  2. Angelica - Angelica is a perfect fit for this team due to her ability to strip debuffs. Ensure she has at least 105 Effectiveness. Equip her with Spirit’s Breath for optimal performance.

  3. Asseria - Asseria’s artifact choice is flexible, but aim for 105 Effectiveness. This will guarantee successful debuffing. Avoid using the “Song of Stars” artifact, as it may trigger the Queen’s self-cleanse ability.

  4. Specter Tenebria - Specter Tenebria is a staple unit in this team composition. Make sure to equip her with Caldera and adjust her artifact for this specific fight.

  5. Guardian - The choice of Guardian is up to you, as you won’t be using it during the fight. Any Guardian will suffice.

Preparing for the Fight#

Before engaging in the fight, it’s important to ensure that all your skills are ready. Turn off the auto mode or manually use only S1 skills in the final fight to conserve your skills. Before entering the Queen’s lair, heal your team, and drink potions to reach optimal health. Consider camping to boost your morale for an extra 5% health.

The Fight#

  1. Start the fight by using Tamarin’s S3 to initiate the battle with the Queen. Avoid using any other skills at this stage, as the Queen will self-cleanse them when she transforms.

  2. When the bees hatch, they will inflict debuffs. However, Tamarin’s transformation will cleanse these debuffs. Use Tamarin’s S2 to push everyone’s CR by 50% and allow them to cycle off the Queen’s binding.

  3. Use Specter Tenebria’s S1 to strip the debuffs after a dual attack with Tamarin.

  4. Reset Tamarin with Asseria’s S3 as soon as she comes out of Idol’s form. Use Tamarin’s S3 to apply buff block and defense breaks on the Queen.

  5. Focus all your attacks on the Queen, while ignoring the bees, to bring her health as close to 50% as possible without triggering phase 2.

  6. Utilize the Arius effect by attacking the bees. This will indirectly lower the Queen’s health without triggering the next phase.

  7. Keep chipping away at the bees and maintain Tamarin’s S2 for healing and Specter Tenebria’s defense breaks. Aim to keep the Queen’s buff block active with Angelica’s S3.

  8. When the Queen’s health is around 15%, prepare for phase 2 by ensuring all your units are healthy and buff block is active.

  9. Transition into phase 2 by using Tamarin’s S3. Remember not to attack the Queen while she has buffs, as it will trigger counter-attacks. Use Tamarin’s S2 for additional healing.

  10. Finally, wait for the Queen’s special buff to drop and continue your attacks until victory is achieved.

Conclusion#

Congratulations! You have successfully cleared the Nightmare Asthma Callous raid with your ultimate 100% clear team. Remember, if you don’t have specific units mentioned in this article, feel free to explore alternative team compositions in the provided links.

With this new knowledge, you can take on the raid with confidence and make your monthly grind in Epic Seven much easier. Good luck and happy raiding!
