Introduction#

Welcome back to our channel! In this video, we will be exploring the next part of the Golden Slumber questline in Genshin Impact. Specifically, we will be focusing on the Dreams Beneath the Searing Sand quest. As you progress through the previous quests, this one will automatically trigger. Join us as we navigate through this captivating world and uncover the secrets of the Searing Sand.

Step 1: Returning to the Oasis#

To begin this quest, your first task is to return to the Oasis. If you don’t have a specific weapon required for this part, don’t worry, you can proceed without it. Head over to the designated rest point as your next destination.

Step 2: Exploring the Mysterious Place#

Upon arriving at the rest point, you’ll notice that this place is beautifully decorated and hides a Teleport Waypoint. It may not be immediately visible due to the icon, but rest assured, it’s there. From the rest point, make your way to the next location.

Step 3: Searching for the Kajini Suit#

Your objective now is to search for the Kajini Suit. Look around the area until you find a book. Unfortunately, you cannot interact with it. This indicates that the clues you need are somewhere else. Keep searching until you find the right one.

Step 4: Completing the Three Trials#

Get ready for the three trials you need to pass. Start with the first one, then move on to the second and third trials. Each trial requires specific actions and navigation. Follow the steps carefully to ensure success. Remember, every move matters, and a wrong step could lead you astray.

Step 5: Activating the Symbols#

As you progress through the trials, you’ll encounter symbols that are crucial to opening the hidden doors. Pay close attention to any symbols you find on your path. The symbols on your character’s head provide clues about which doors will open. Utilize this information to ensure you progress correctly.

Step 6: Finding the Keystones#

During your exploration, you’ll come across the need to find Keystones. Search diligently for these items, as they are vital for unlocking certain doors. Keep an eye out for enemies and chests that may hold the Keystones you need.

Step 7: Reveal the Invisible Path#

One interesting feature of this quest is the invisible walls that become visible through manipulation. Follow the instructions and activate the necessary devices to reveal the hidden walls. This will allow you to access new areas and continue your journey.

Step 8: Unlocking the Final Door#

After successfully navigating through trials, solving puzzles, and unlocking various doors, you’ll finally encounter the last door. Activate the proper mechanisms to open this door and move forward.

Step 9: Confronting the Final Challenge#

Approach the magnificent throne that awaits you beyond the final door. Prepare for a tough fight as you confront the challenge that lies in wait. Defeat the enemy and emerge victorious.

Conclusion#

Congratulations! By following these steps, you have completed the Dreams Beneath the Searing Sand quest in Genshin Impact. You have conquered the trials, solved the puzzles, and faced formidable enemies along the way. Remember, there is more to explore in this vast world, and new quests will be waiting for you. Continue your journey and enjoy the wonders and challenges that Genshin Impact has to offer. Thank you for watching, and we’ll see you in the next video!
