In the world of Summoners War, one monster stands out as a force to be reckoned with in the Real-Time Arena Special League: Shimitae, the Wind Sylph. This powerful monster possesses abilities and skills that make it an invaluable asset in battles, both for beginners and experienced players. In this article, we will delve into the details of Shimitae’s abilities, its potential in RTA Special League, and how it can be a game-changer in your Summoners War journey.

Introduction to Shimitae, the Wind Sylph#

Shimitae is a natural 4-star monster belonging to the Wind attribute. Its unique skill set and high base speed make it an ideal choice for battles that prioritize speed and crowd control. With its striking design and swift attacks, Shimitae is truly a force to be reckoned within the Summoners War universe.

Key Points:#

  • Shimitae is a Wind Sylph with high base speed and valuable abilities.
  • Its unique skill set makes it suitable for battles that prioritize speed and crowd control.

Skill Analysis#

  1. Whirlwind:

    • Attacks all enemies with a tornado, causing damage proportionate to Shimitae’s attack power and decreases the Attack Bar of each target by 30%. This skill also has a 40% chance to inflict Continuous Damage for 2 turns.
    • The cooldown for Whirlwind is 4 turns when maxed.
    • This skill allows Shimitae to unleash a devastating attack on all enemies, decreasing their Attack Bar and potentially inflicting Continuous Damage. It can quickly turn the tide of battle in your favor.

  2. Slicing Winds:

    • Attacks all enemies with a gust of wind, inflicting damage proportionate to Shimitae’s attack power. Additionally, this skill has a 30% chance to stun the enemies for 1 turn.
    • The cooldown for Slicing Winds is 4 turns when maxed.
    • This skill provides Shimitae with crowd control abilities, as it can stun enemies, allowing for more strategic gameplay and giving you the upper hand in battles.

  3. Forceful Wind (Passive):

    • Increases Shimitae’s Attack Speed by 15% for each harmful effect on the enemies, up to a maximum of 3 harmful effects. Additionally, when Shimitae successfully lands a Critical Hit, his Attack Bar is increased by 20%.
    • This passive skill enhances Shimitae’s speed and attack potential as battles progress, making it a formidable threat in the later stages of a match.

Key Points:#

  • Shimitae’s Whirlwind skill deals AOE damage, decreases the Attack Bar, and has a chance to apply Continuous Damage.
  • Slicing Winds inflicts damage on all enemies and has a chance to stun them.
  • Forceful Wind (passive) increases Shimitae’s Attack Speed and Attack Bar based on harmful effects and Critical Hits.

Utilizing Shimitae in RTA Special League#

In the Real-Time Arena Special League, speed and crowd control play crucial roles in securing victories. Shimitae’s unique skill set perfectly aligns with these requirements, making it a top choice for players aiming to climb the ranks. Here’s how you can effectively utilize Shimitae in your RTA Special League battles:

Step 1: Building the Right Team#

To maximize Shimitae’s potential, it is essential to build a team that complements its speed and crowd control abilities. Consider the following monsters to form a strong team:

  • Bastet: A Water Desert Queen that can increase the Attack Bar of your team and provide beneficial effects.
  • Gany: A Wind Sky Dancer that can reset skill cooldowns and grant additional turns.
  • Mo Long: An Earth Panda Warrior that can disrupt the enemy team with heavy damage and crowd control.

Step 2: Speed Tuning#

Speed tuning your team is crucial to ensure Shimitae’s abilities take effect at the right time. Make sure to adjust the Speed stats of your monsters so that Shimitae can make the most of its crowd control skills.

Step 3: Strategy in Battle#

During battles, focus on using Shimitae’s crowd control skills to stun the enemy team and decrease their Attack Bar. This will give you enough control over the battlefield to plan your moves strategically. Coordinate with your team to target the most threatening enemies and eliminate them swiftly.

Step 4: Modifying Shimitae’s Runes#

To further enhance Shimitae’s potential, make sure to equip it with appropriate runes. Swift runes that boost Speed are recommended to increase its turn cycle. Focus on increasing its Attack stat to amplify the damage output of its skills. Additionally, consider using Despair runes to increase the chances of stunning enemies.

Key Points:#

  • Build a team that complements Shimitae’s speed and crowd control abilities, consider monsters like Bastet, Gany, and Mo Long.
  • Speed tuning your team ensures Shimitae’s abilities take effect at the right time.
  • Use Shimitae’s crowd control skills strategically to stun enemies and decrease their Attack Bar.
  • Modify Shimitae’s runes with Swift and Despair sets to maximize its potential.

Conclusion#

Shimitae, the Wind Sylph, is a true powerhouse in Summoners War’s RTA Special League. With its crowd control skills, high base speed, and ability to inflict damage, it can turn the tide of battle in your favor. By incorporating Shimitae into your team and utilizing its unique skill set effectively, you can greatly increase your chances of success in the highly competitive RTA Special League. So, seize the power of Shimitae, and may victory be yours in Summoners War!
